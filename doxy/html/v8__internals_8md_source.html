23 ### NAN_GC_CALLBACK(callbackname)
24 
25 Use `NAN_GC_CALLBACK` to declare your callbacks for `Nan::AddGCPrologueCallback()` and `Nan::AddGCEpilogueCallback()`. Your new method receives the arguments `v8::GCType type` and `v8::GCCallbackFlags flags`.
26 
27 ```c++
28 static Nan::Persistent<Function> callback;
29 
30 NAN_GC_CALLBACK(gcPrologueCallback) {
31  v8::Local<Value> argv[] = { Nan::New("prologue").ToLocalChecked() };
32  Nan::MakeCallback(Nan::GetCurrentContext()->Global(), Nan::New(callback), 1, argv);
33 }
34 
35 NAN_METHOD(Hook) {
36  callback.Reset(To<Function>(args[0]).ToLocalChecked());
37  Nan::AddGCPrologueCallback(gcPrologueCallback);
38  info.GetReturnValue().Set(info.Holder());
39 }
40 ```
